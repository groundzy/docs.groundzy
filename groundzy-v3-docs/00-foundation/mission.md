# Mission

**Groundzy helps people and organizations care for trees using a map-centered, data-rich web platform**—so inventory, context, and action stay connected.

We serve:

- **Property stewards** — Homeowners and others who need to **see**, **record**, and **understand** the trees they are responsible for, with limits and capabilities matched to **Home**, **Plus**, and related entry tiers.
- **Tree care professionals** — Arborists, landscapers, students, and related roles who need **stronger limits**, **export**, **clients and properties**, and operational tooling at **Pro** and aligned tiers.
- **Teams and organizations** — Businesses and institutions that need **collaboration**, **administration**, **dashboards**, and an end-to-end **requests → quotes → jobs → invoices** pipeline, with team-sized plans where the product provides them.
- **Those seeking help** — Where the product includes it, pathways to **find and contact** tree care professionals (e.g. surfaces aimed at homeowners, not at the pro operating the CRM).

Our mission is not to ship isolated features. It is to deliver **one coherent product** in which map, trees, AI, weather, CRM, workflow, billing, and sharing reinforce each other—grounded in domain concepts the legacy app already proved (see repository `docs/PROJECT_OVERVIEW.md` and `docs/features/`).

If a capability does not clearly serve these audiences or strengthen this spine, it does not belong in the mission by default.
