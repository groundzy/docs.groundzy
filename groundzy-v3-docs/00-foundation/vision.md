# Vision

Groundzy exists to be the **definitive software layer for smart tree care**—connecting what lives on the land with what people and organizations **know**, **plan**, and **do** about it.

Long-term, Groundzy aims to:

- **Unify the tree care lifecycle** — From inventory and health insight on a map through professional service, billing, and ongoing stewardship—without forcing users to stitch together spreadsheets, photos, and disconnected tools.
- **Scale from property to organization** — The same conceptual world supports a homeowner mapping a yard, a solo arborist running a route, and a crew operating with clients, jobs, and compliance—through **clear tiers and permissions**, not parallel products.
- **Embed intelligence responsibly** — AI and weather augment judgment; they do not replace domain reality. The platform favors **explainable, auditable** assistance tied to real trees and places.
- **Build an ecosystem, not a feature pile** — Partners, pros, and property owners meet in workflows that feel like **one system**, with shared language for trees, work, and outcomes.

**Groundzy v3** is how we **realize that vision in architecture and experience**: a deliberate rebuild so the product can grow for years without re-accumulating the fragmentation that incremental delivery left behind in the legacy app.

This vision is **directional**. Sequencing, markets, and technical choices are decided in roadmap and architecture work—not in this file alone.
